Create a more sustainable future with SWAI

SWAI is a mobile application that promotes a change of behavior about sustainability and environment awareness.

SWAI utilizes the concept of gamification in order to make it fun to learn about and engage with the importance of recycling, composting, and waste separation.

All this while giving users basic education about what we should be doing with our waste. It’s available for download in google play (available only in Indonesia).

Why Gringgo?

SWAI urges users to collect different necessary data through the utilization of missions (gamification) and the token economy concept (users can collect points and redeem attractive incentives). The app also provides users with educational content regarding waste segregation
