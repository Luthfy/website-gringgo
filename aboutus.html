- Our Story -

Gringgo started in August 2015 as a cash for garbage project by some of the founding members. The main goal was to use digital technology to improve an existing waste management system in a country where trash management is still insufficient. Gringgo used GIS and mapping as a strength to generate better data while innovating in web and mobile technology.

On September 25, 2015, world leaders endorsed the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which aim to safeguard the environment, combat poverty, and eradicate it in order to create a more prosperous world for future generations. These objectives were set forth in the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

Gringgo is inspired by this and is attempting to bridge the gap by utilizing technical innovation to develop solutions for concerns related to sustainable development. Delivering environmental acts, offering reliable information, creating opportunities, and providing technical aid to minimize societal inequalities.
