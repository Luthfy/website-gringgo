Sustainability Compliance

One of our projects, Waste Compliance: Waste management compliance involves important but complex regulations regarding the handling and disposal of universal, particular and hazardous wastes. Gringgo offers solutions for companies looking to meet standards in waste management.

Education for sustainable development (ESD) facilitates the development of the knowledge, skills, awareness, values, and activities necessary to build a world that is environmentally friendly, promotes social fairness, and is economically viable. As part of the community that care, Gringgo conducted Waste Separation Education in 203 cities in Indonesia with 25,000 new users SWAi App by end 2022
