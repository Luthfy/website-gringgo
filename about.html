Our Story

Gringgo started in August 2015 as a Cash for Trash project by some of the founding members. The main goal was to use digital technology to improve an existing waste management system in a country where trash management is still insufficient.

Charity Arm

In 2017, Gringgo Indonesia Foundation was founded as a charity arm to deliver impact-based programs for the community.

Strategic

In 2020, Gringgo rebranded with a new logo and new looks. Gringgo saw opportunities to expand the development of it's products not only in the waste management industry, but also to serve other sustainable development issues.
